The CMC Wireless Report, a bimonthly journal, is sold only by subscription. Subscriptions are sold to individuals who may quote small portions of the text, but this publication is copyrighted and copies, in print or electronic form may not be shared without permission of CMC Associates.

Subscriptions are annual with new reports issued approximately every two months, based on the frequency of ISA100 and Working Group meetings. The subject matter relates to industrial wireless standardization and the commercial market resulting from this work. Each Report will relate to some aspect of the emerging standards, commercial product introduction, and related technologies. Subscribers will occasionally receive additional reports of interest to the general industrial automation market.
